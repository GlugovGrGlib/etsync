## Top 10 Highest-Impact Actions (Priority Order)

1. **Fix your primary listing photos** — lifestyle/mockup shots, not flat product-on-white. This single change has the biggest effect on click-through rate, which drives Etsy's quality score and all downstream ranking.

2. **Rewrite titles to the 2026 formula** — under 15 words, primary product noun first, then 3 objective descriptors. Remove filler words ("beautiful," "unique," "stunning"). Do NOT touch titles on listings that already rank well.

3. **Use all 13 tags as multi-word phrases** — 3-4 broad tags + 8-9 long-tail tags. Never repeat words from your title. Never use single-word tags.

4. **Price in USD, build shipping into the price** — list your shop currency as USD. Absorb shipping cost into item price for items where shipping is <20% of total. Show "free shipping" to US buyers.

5. **Set up Etsy's automated coupons today** — abandoned cart coupons (10-15% off) and thank-you coupons (10% off next order). These are built into Etsy and convert immediately.

6. **Add video to every listing** — even a 5-second slow pan across the product. Square or vertical format. Mobile drives 46% of Etsy app sales.

7. **Start posting on Pinterest** — 5-15 pins/day across 10-15 keyword-rich boards. Pin your products AND complementary content. Pinterest is the #1 external traffic source for art/decor Etsy shops and pins have months-long lifespan.

8. **Use Packlink or Sendcloud for shipping** — compare rates across Correos, DHL, GLS, DPD per shipment. Saves 15-30% vs. single carrier. Use DHL for Germany-bound orders (German buyers trust it).

9. **Lean into "Handmade in Spain"** — Mediterranean aesthetic is trending in Northern EU. Show your studio, local materials, Mediterranean settings. Write your About page. This is your moat against AI art and mass production.

10. **Create gallery wall bundles and size tiers** — bundle 3-5 prints at 15-25% discount vs. individual. Offer every design in 3 sizes. The medium option sells most. Both tactics increase average order value.

## Key Numbers to Know

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Etsy transaction fee | 6.5% of sale price + shipping |
| Payment processing (EU) | ~4% + EUR 0.30 |
| Offsite Ads fee | 15% (under EUR 10K/yr), 12% (over EUR 10K/yr) |
| Currency conversion fee | ~2.5% |
| Effective total fee (no Offsite Ads) | 11-14% |
| Effective total fee (with Offsite Ads) | 26-29% |
| Listing fee | $0.20 per 4 months |
| Germany LUCID registration | Mandatory if shipping to DE. Free reg, ~20-100 EUR/yr dual system. |
| France CITEO/PRO registration | Mandatory if shipping to FR. ~80-150 EUR/yr. |
| GPSR responsible person | Mandatory on all EU listings since Dec 2024. |
| US tariffs on EU goods | Reciprocal tariffs on EU goods — rates change frequently. Verify current rate at hts.usitc.gov before selling to US. |
| Star Seller requirements | 95% response in 24h, 95% on-time tracked shipping, 4.8+ star average, min 5 orders/$300 in 3 months |
| Title limit | 140 chars, aim for <15 words |
| Tags | 13 slots, 20 chars each |
| Photos | Up to 20, min 2000px on shortest side |
| Video | 1 per listing, 5-15 sec, max 100MB |
| Alt text | 250 chars per image |

## Pricing Formula

```
Item Price = (Materials x 3) + (Hours x Your Hourly Rate) + Shipping Cost + Fee Buffer

Fee Buffer = Item Price x 0.15  (covers Etsy fees)
             Item Price x 0.30  (if Offsite Ads can apply)
```

For art/sculpture, target a minimum 3-4x materials+labor multiplier to cover fees, marketing time, and profit.
