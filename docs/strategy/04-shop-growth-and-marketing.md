## Social Media: Which Platforms and How

### Platform Priority (for art/sculpture/handmade/garden decor)

| Platform | Priority | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Pinterest** | #1 | Highest ROI. Visual search engine with purchase intent. Pins last months-years. Core Etsy demographic overlap. |
| **TikTok** | #2 | Highest viral potential. Best for reaching 25-35 age group. Process videos perform extremely well. |
| **Instagram** | #3 | Best for community building and repeat customers. Reels essential. |
| **YouTube** | #4 | Growing for long-form behind-the-scenes. Strong SEO value. |
| **Facebook** | #5 | Declining organically, but niche Groups still valuable. |

**Rule:** post consistently on 1-2 platforms rather than sporadically on five. 3-4x/week on your primary platform beats daily posting on everything.

### Content Mix

| Content Type | % of Posts | Examples |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Process/Creation** | 40% | Time-lapse painting, sculpting, kiln loading, studio time, WIP |
| **Product showcase** | 20% | Styled photos, flat lays, lifestyle shots, art on wall |
| **Personal/Story** | 20% | Why you create, your journey, challenges, artist life |
| **Educational** | 10% | How to frame prints, how to style decor, art care tips |
| **Social proof** | 10% | Customer photos, reviews, unboxing reactions |

### Short-Form Video Formats That Convert

These formats consistently drive Etsy traffic for art/handmade sellers:

1. **"Watch me make this"** — 15-60 sec time-lapse, finished product reveal at end. Use trending audio.
2. **"Pack an order with me"** — ASMR-style packing. Tissue paper, stamps, thank-you cards. Enormously popular.
3. **Before/After** — blank canvas to finished painting, raw clay to glazed sculpture.
4. **"Things in my shop under $X"** — quick slideshow of affordable items.
5. **"POV: you ordered from my small business"** — shows care in each order.
6. **"I made this because..."** — story-driven content about inspiration.

**Technical requirements:**
- Hook viewers in first 1-2 seconds — start with the most visual moment
- Text overlays always (many watch without sound)
- Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- 15-30 seconds optimal, up to 60 for process videos
- Natural light > expensive equipment
- Say "link in bio" — never put direct links in captions (algorithms suppress them)
- 3-5 relevant hashtags, not 30

### Pinterest Strategy (Your #1 Traffic Source)

**Pinterest is a search engine, not a social network. Treat it like SEO.**

**Pin optimization:**
- **Titles:** keyword-rich natural phrases. "Minimalist Watercolor Print Ocean Wall Art" not "Beautiful New Piece!"
- **Descriptions:** 2-3 sentences with keywords. "This minimalist ocean watercolor brings calm coastal vibes to any room. Perfect for beach house decor or a thoughtful housewarming gift."
- **Image:** 2:3 vertical ratio (1000x1500px). Bright, clean. Text overlay optional.
- **Alt text:** describe the image with keywords

**Board strategy:**
- Create 10-15 themed boards: "Ocean Wall Art," "Living Room Gallery Ideas," "Gifts for Art Lovers," "Modern Garden Sculpture," etc.
- Board titles and descriptions should be keyword-rich
- Pin your products AND relevant third-party content (70/30 ratio)
- Pin 5-15 pins/day, spread throughout the day (use Tailwind or Pinterest's scheduler)

**Timing:**
- Start pinning Christmas content in August/September
- Valentine's content in November
- Spring garden content in January
- Pinterest predicts trends months ahead — ride the wave early

**Keyword research:** use Pinterest's search bar autocomplete — these are actual search terms. Check trends.pinterest.com for rising keywords.

## Shop Optimization

### Shop Sections

Create 5-15 sections organized by how customers browse:

**By product type:** "Original Paintings," "Art Prints," "Sculpture," "Garden Decor," "Digital Downloads"
**By subject:** "Ocean & Coastal," "Botanical," "Abstract," "Landscapes"
**By use case:** "Living Room Wall Art," "Bedroom Decor," "Outdoor Art," "Gift Sets"

- Section names contribute to shop SEO — include keywords
- Put best-selling sections first
- Rotate seasonal sections to the top (holiday items in November, garden in spring)

### About Page

Your About page directly affects conversion. Complete it fully:

- [ ] **Personal story** — why you create, how you started, what inspires you. First person, conversational.
- [ ] **Photos of yourself** working in your studio
- [ ] **Workshop/studio photos** — humanizes the shop, differentiates from mass production
- [ ] **Process photos or video** — you creating work
- [ ] **Qualifications** (if relevant) — art education, exhibitions, press features
- [ ] **Location** mentioned positively — "Handcrafted in my studio in [city, country]"

Etsy's algorithm boosts shops with complete About sections.

### Shop Announcement

Update monthly with:
- Current processing times
- New arrivals / seasonal collections
- Upcoming sales or commission deadlines
- Holiday shipping cutoffs

Active shops rank higher. An outdated announcement signals an abandoned shop.

### Shop Policies for International Sellers

Write clear policies that build trust with US buyers:

**Shipping:**
```
Processing time: [X] business days
US delivery estimate: [X-X] business days (standard) / [X-X] business days (express)
Carrier: DHL eCommerce (standard), DHL Express (express upgrade)
All orders ship with tracking.
International buyers may be responsible for customs duties upon delivery.
```

**Returns:**
```
I accept returns within 14 days of delivery for prints and decor items.
Original artwork: exchanges only (contact me first).
Buyer pays return shipping.
If your item arrives damaged, photograph the damage AND packaging within 48 hours.
```

**Custom orders:**
```
I accept commissions. Processing time: [X] weeks.
50% non-refundable deposit required to begin.
1-2 revisions included. Additional revisions at hourly rate.
Holiday commission deadline: November 1.
```

## Email Marketing

### Etsy's Built-In Tools

- **Abandoned cart coupons** — set 10-15% off. Automatically sent to buyers who added items but didn't purchase. High conversion rate. **Set this up today.**
- **Thank-you coupons** — 10% off next order, automatically sent after purchase. Drives repeat business.
- **Favoriter offers** — send a coupon to people who favorited your items. Converts interest to sales.
- **Etsy's email marketing** — send emails to past buyers (who opted in). Use around key events: Black Friday, new collections, holiday shipping deadlines.

### Building an External Email List

Etsy's tools are limited. For long-term independence from any platform, build your own list:

- Use Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or ConvertKit (free tiers available)
- Offer a freebie to build your list: downloadable phone wallpaper, mini print, or discount code
- Collect emails through social media, your website, or a QR code in your packaging
- Send 2-4 emails/month: new launches, behind-the-scenes, exclusive sales
- **Email has the highest ROI of any marketing channel** — you own the relationship

## Sales, Coupons, and Promotions

### Promotion Types

| Type | When to Use | Discount |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Abandoned cart coupons | Always on (automated) | 10-15% |
| Thank-you coupons | Always on (automated) | 10% next order |
| Favoriter offers | Monthly or during slow periods | 10-15% |
| Shop-wide sale | Black Friday, major holidays only | 15-25% |
| Slow-moving inventory sale | Items unsold for 3-6 months | 20-30% |

### Rules

- **Do not run sales constantly** — trains buyers to wait for discounts, devalues your brand
- **Align sales with shopping events**: Black Friday/Cyber Monday, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, Etsy-wide sale events
- **Price products so you can afford 15-20% off** without losing money
- **Use tiered promotions**: "Buy 2, get 10% off; Buy 3, get 20% off" — increases order value
- **Include a physical coupon card in every package** — 10-15% off next order with a unique code. Low-tech, high-impact.

## Bundling and Upselling

### Tactics That Work for Art/Decor

1. **Gallery wall sets** — bundle 3-5 prints that work together. Price at 15-25% less than individual. High perceived value, high average order value.
2. **Size upgrades** — offer 3-4 sizes. The medium-large sells most. Price the small attractively so medium looks like good value.
3. **Room-ready bundles** — "Living Room Art Collection" of complementary pieces.
4. **Frame options** — if you can offer framed versions, the markup is significant and buyers appreciate the convenience.
5. **Matching products** — sell art? Add matching phone cases, tote bags, notebooks via print-on-demand (Printify/Printful). No inventory needed.
6. **Gift wrapping add-on** — small charge, high margin around holidays.
7. **Custom variations** — custom colors, sizes, or personalization for a premium.

## Customer Engagement and Repeat Buyers

### Post-Sale Sequence

1. **Handwritten thank-you note in every package** — single highest-ROI action for generating reviews and repeat purchases
2. **Physical coupon card** — 10-15% off next order
3. **Etsy message 2-3 weeks after delivery:** "I hope you're enjoying your [item]. If you have a moment, I'd love a review. Follow my shop for new releases." Brief. Not pushy.
4. **Automated thank-you coupon** via Etsy (10% off next order)

### Getting More Reviews

- The unboxing experience drives reviews — invest in it (tissue paper, branded stickers, care card)
- Follow up via Etsy message after delivery confirmation — opens communication before problems escalate
- Resolve issues proactively — most negative reviews come from poor communication, not poor products
- Ship on time or early — late shipments are the #1 negative review trigger

### Building Repeat Business

- Make it easy to follow your shop — mention it in thank-you notes and social media
- Launch collections (not just individual pieces) — gives buyers reasons to come back
- Share new work on social media consistently — keeps you top of mind
- Use Etsy's favoriter offers to re-engage people who showed interest

## Handling the Slow Season (Jan-Feb, Mid-Summer)

1. **Optimize existing listings** — update photos, rewrite descriptions, refresh tags
2. **Create new listings** — the new listing boost helps during slow periods
3. **Batch-create content** for the upcoming busy season
4. **Prepare Q4 holiday inventory** (start in summer)
5. **Run targeted promotions** — abandoned cart and favoriter offers work well during slow periods
6. **Experiment** — test new product types, price points, keywords
7. **Build your email list and social following** — invest in long-term traffic sources
8. **Reach out to boutiques** for wholesale (via Faire or direct)

## Etsy Fees and Pricing Strategy

### Fee Structure (2025)

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Listing fee | $0.20 per 4 months (auto-renews) |
| Transaction fee | 6.5% of (item price + shipping) |
| Payment processing (EU) | ~4% + EUR 0.30 |
| Currency conversion | ~2.5% (if shop currency differs from payout currency) |
| Offsite Ads (under $10K/yr) | 15% — can opt out |
| Offsite Ads (over $10K/yr) | 12% — cannot opt out |

**Effective total fee: 11-14% without Offsite Ads, 26-29% with.**

### Pricing Checklist

- [ ] Materials cost calculated (including packaging)
- [ ] Labor valued at $25-50/hour minimum
- [ ] Shipping cost covered (absorbed or charged)
- [ ] Etsy fees covered (add 15% buffer minimum, 30% if Offsite Ads apply)
- [ ] Margin for 15-20% seasonal sales built in
- [ ] Competitive with similar items on Etsy (check total price including shipping)
- [ ] Premium positioning justified by story, quality, origin

## 2025-2026 Marketplace Trends

### Algorithm and Platform

- **Video-first mobile experience** — Etsy app drives 46% of sales, video auto-plays on hover in desktop search
- **NLP/semantic search** — Etsy understands meaning, not just keywords. Write naturally.
- **Shipping cost matters for ranking** — keep under $6 or absorb into price
- **Search Visibility Page** — check it regularly, fix flagged issues

### Buyer Behavior

- **Anti-AI backlash = opportunity** — buyers actively seek verifiably human-made art. Show your process. Say "100% hand-painted." Document your creation.
- **"Buy less, buy better"** — anti-mass-production sentiment favors handmade. Market as investment pieces, not disposable goods.
- **Sustainability is expected, not optional** — eco-friendly packaging, sustainable materials, mention it in listings and tags
- **Maximalism returning** — bold colors, layered gallery walls, eclectic mixing after years of neutral minimalism

### What Etsy Is Pushing

- Star Seller badge (modest ranking boost, requires 95% on all metrics)
- Complete shop profiles (About page, policies, shop story)
- Video on listings
- Free or low-cost shipping
- Multi-language listings for international reach

### Protecting Your Business

- **Build an email list** — you own it, independent of any platform
- **Start your own website** (Shopify, Squarespace) as a secondary channel — reduces Etsy dependency
- **Consider Faire for wholesale** — if you can produce at volume
- **Document everything** — process photos, receipts, customs forms. Protects against disputes.

## Star Seller: Worth It?

### Requirements

- 95%+ messages responded to within 24 hours
- 95%+ orders shipped on time with tracking
- 4.8+ star average rating
- Minimum 5 orders and $300 in rolling 3 months

### Verdict

**Aim for it if your business model naturally supports it. Do not make decisions that hurt profitability or mental health just to maintain the badge.**

The ranking boost is modest. The behaviors it requires (fast responses, on-time shipping) are good business practices anyway. The 4.8-star average requirement is more forgiving than the old 95% five-star rule, but still partially outside your control. For international sellers, the tracking requirement can be expensive for small items.

The fundamentals (great products, excellent photos, good SEO, reliable shipping) matter far more than the badge.
