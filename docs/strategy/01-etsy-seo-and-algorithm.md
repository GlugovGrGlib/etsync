# Etsy SEO & Algorithm Guide (2025-2026)

## How Etsy Search Works

Etsy search is a two-phase process:

1. **Query matching** — determines if your listing is eligible to appear. Etsy checks titles, tags, categories, attributes, and description keywords. If none match the search query, your listing never enters the ranking pool.
2. **Ranking** — eligible listings are scored and ordered using performance signals and buyer personalization.

### Key Ranking Factors

| Factor | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| Listing Quality Score | Click-through rate, favorites, add-to-cart, purchases |
| Customer & Market Experience | Reviews, shipping reliability, case history, response time |
| Recency | New/renewed listings get a 24-72 hour boost |
| Conversion Rate | Views-to-purchases ratio |

Results are personalized per buyer based on style preferences, price sensitivity, location, and engagement history.

### 2025-2026 Algorithm Changes

1. **Shipping over $6 penalizes ranking** — a $22 mug with free shipping outranks a $15 mug with $8 shipping. Action: absorb shipping into price wherever possible.
2. **Keyword-stuffed titles are discouraged** — Etsy recommends natural language titles under 15 words. No active penalty, but the Search Visibility Page may flag poorly written titles.
3. **NLP/Semantic matching is live** — Etsy uses LLMs to understand meaning, not just keywords. "Boho wedding dress" matches "bohemian bridal gown." "Cozy knitted blanket" appears for "hygge home decor."
4. **Video listings get priority** — auto-play on hover in desktop search. Mobile = 46% of Etsy app sales.
5. **Search Visibility Page** — shows which listings have reduced visibility and why (title quality, shipping price, missing attributes, photo quality).

## Title Optimization

### Rules

- **140 character limit**, but only the first 40-50 characters show on mobile and Google
- **Keep under 15 words** (2026 guidance)
- **First 3-5 words must be the primary product noun** — what the item literally is
- Use **commas** to separate keyword phrases
- Use **title case** (never ALL CAPS)

### Formula

```
[Primary Product Noun] + [Key Attributes: color, size, material] + [Style/Use Case]
```

### Do This

```
Textured Abstract Painting, Earth Tone Wall Art, Modern Living Room Decor
Hand-Forged Metal Garden Sculpture, Corten Steel Yard Art, Outdoor Decor
Ceramic Planter Pot, Minimalist Concrete Plant Holder, Indoor Garden
```

### Do NOT Do This

```
Beautiful Unique Stunning Wall Art Perfect Gift For Her Home Decor Amazing
FREE SHIPPING *** Best Seller *** Handmade Art *** Limited Time
```

### What to Remove from Titles

- Filler adjectives: "beautiful," "unique," "stunning," "perfect," "amazing"
- Sales info: "free shipping," "best seller," "limited time," "sale"
- Occasion keywords — move these to tags instead ("gift for him," "Mother's Day")
- Symbols, emojis, stars, hearts

### Critical Rule

Do NOT change titles on listings that are already ranking well. Only rewrite underperforming listings.

## Tag Optimization

### Limits

- 13 tag slots — **use every single one**
- 20 characters max per tag
- 260 total characters across all tags

### Strategy: Balance Broad and Specific

- **3-4 broad tags** for visibility: "wall art," "home decor," "garden sculpture"
- **8-9 long-tail tags** for conversion: "abstract earth tone painting," "metal yard art rustic"

### Rules

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use multi-word phrases (3-5 words) | Use single-word tags ("art," "blue") |
| Add synonyms not in your title | Repeat words from your title |
| Include regional variations ("gray" if title says "grey") | Duplicate words across tags |
| Use all 13 slots | Leave any slot empty |
| Match what buyers actually search | Use intentional misspellings |

### Tag Examples for Art/Sculpture/Decor

**Art pieces:**
```
abstract earth tone art    | botanical print decor      | minimalist line drawing
textured canvas painting   | modern gallery wall art     | nature inspired artwork
large living room art      | original acrylic painting   | contemporary wall decor
```

**Sculpture:**
```
modern metal sculpture     | abstract table sculpture    | ceramic sculptural art
handmade bronze figure     | garden statue outdoor       | kinetic wind art
```

**Garden decor:**
```
metal garden sculpture     | outdoor yard art rustic     | garden stake flower
kinetic wind spinner       | concrete planter modern     | fairy garden decor
```

### When to Change Tags

Review Shop Stats monthly. If a listing gets impressions but no clicks — your photo is the problem, not tags. If it gets no impressions — your tags need work.

## Description Optimization

Since May 2022, Etsy uses description keywords for query matching. Since 2025, NLP means Etsy understands context, not just exact keywords.

### Structure

```
[First 160 characters: primary keyword phrase + what the item is + who it's for]

[2-3 sentences: story, inspiration, what makes it special]

[Bullet points: dimensions, materials, weight, care instructions]

[Shipping info: processing time, carrier, delivery estimate]

[Call to action: "Message me for custom sizes" or "Follow my shop for new releases"]
```

### First 160 Characters Are Critical

This appears as the Google meta-description. Include your primary product description naturally.

**Good:** "Hand-textured abstract painting on stretched canvas, 24x36 inches. Warm earth tones bring organic calm to any living room or office space."

**Bad:** "This stunning, unique, beautiful one-of-a-kind tapestry is perfect for anyone who loves art and wants something special for their home."

### Rules

- Write for humans first, search second
- Use your natural brand voice
- Do not copy your title verbatim into the description
- Do not keyword-stuff — it hurts credibility and conversion
- Mention key details (materials, sizing) in the first few sentences
- For art: mention medium, surface, whether it's stretched/unstretched, framed/unframed

## Photo & Video Requirements

### Photos

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Maximum photos | 20 per listing (use at least 5-7) |
| Minimum resolution | 2000px on shortest side (3000 x 2250 recommended for first photo) |
| File size | Under 1 MB |
| Formats | .jpg, .gif, .png |
| Aspect ratio | Square (1:1) recommended |

### What Each Photo Slot Should Show

1. **Hero shot** — the product, clean and clear, well-lit. This determines your click-through rate.
2. **Lifestyle/context** — art on a wall, sculpture on a shelf, planter in a garden
3. **Scale reference** — next to a hand, ruler, furniture, or common object
4. **Detail/texture close-up** — brushstrokes, glaze, weld marks, material grain
5. **Alternate angle** — back, side, or 3/4 view
6. **Packaging** — shows you care about safe delivery, builds trust
7. **Process/studio shot** — your hands creating, your workspace
8-10. **Additional contexts, color variations, or "before/after" (blank wall vs. with art)**

### Video

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Videos per listing | 1 |
| Duration | 5-15 seconds |
| Max file size | 100 MB |
| Min resolution | 500 px (1080p recommended) |
| Formats | MP4, MOV, AVI |
| Audio | Stripped automatically — videos play silently |
| Best aspect ratio | Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5) — higher engagement than 16:9 on mobile |

**What to show in video:** slow pan across texture, 360-degree rotation (especially sculpture), the piece in its environment.

### Alt Text

- 250 character limit per image
- Describe what's in the photo: color, texture, material, form
- Include 2-3 keywords naturally
- Start with the most critical details
- This helps Etsy's image recognition AND accessibility

## Listing Attributes

Fill out **every single available attribute**. They function as additional tags and determine whether your listing appears in filtered/sidebar searches.

Etsy's Search Visibility Page now flags missing attributes as a reason for reduced visibility. Check it regularly.

Key attributes for art/sculpture/decor: color, material, style, occasion, room, size, technique.

## Etsy Ads

### Onsite Ads

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Starting daily budget | $5/day |
| Minimum evaluation period | 30 days before judging results |
| Target ROAS | $2.00-2.50 return per $1.00 spent |
| Typical CPC | $0.20-0.50 |

**Which listings to advertise:**
- Start with listings that already convert organically (high quality score = lower CPC)
- Focus on items in less saturated sub-categories
- Remove underperformers from campaigns rather than pausing all ads
- Increase budget during peak seasons (Mother's Day, Black Friday, Christmas)

**Do not advertise:**
- Listings with poor photos or low conversion rates (you'll pay for clicks that don't convert)
- Very low-margin items where fees + ad cost eat the profit

### Offsite Ads

| Revenue Tier | Fee | Can Opt Out? |
|-------------|-----|-------------|
| Under $10K/year | 15% | Yes |
| Over $10K/year | 12% | No |

If you cannot opt out, factor the 12-15% into your pricing. The fee applies when a buyer clicks an Etsy offsite ad (Google, Facebook, Instagram) and purchases within 30 days.

## Renewal Strategy

- New/renewed listings get a **24-72 hour recency boost**
- Each renewal costs **$0.20**
- Do not renew everything at once — stagger renewals
- Renew your best performers during peak search periods for their category
- If a listing has been dead for months with views but no sales, **create a fresh listing** with new photos and keywords rather than renewing — the old one has a low quality score

### When to Relist vs. Renew

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Listing gets views but no sales | Rework photos and description, then renew |
| Listing gets no views at all | Rework tags and title, then renew |
| Listing has been dead for 3+ months | Deactivate. Create a fresh listing with new photos, title, tags |
| Listing is performing well | Leave it alone. Do not touch the title. |
